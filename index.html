When Chai Met Toast

About

A chance meeting and an amazing jamming session in Fort Kochi between two musically inclined guys, Achyuth and Ashwin led to the creation of When Chai Met Toast .The band from the coconut land is famous for its chirpy and very uniquely infectious happy music that is sure to make you feel light even when you are having a bad day.
